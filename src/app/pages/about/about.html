About Us

The First Cloud Chapter at SRIT

AWS Cloud Club at SRIT is starting its journey as a community built for the students, by the students. Affiliated with Amazon Web Services, we are dedicated to making cloud computing education accessible, hands-on, and career-ready.

We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice by running real AWS workshops, hackathons, project sprints, and certification prep sessions — all inside your campus.
